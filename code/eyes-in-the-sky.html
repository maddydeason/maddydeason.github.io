Eyes in the Sky

Published: May 2015

This group project was completed as part of our Storytelling with Data Visualization class. We analyzed a large dataset of all active orbiting satellites and determined that China's spy satellite program was rapidly expanding. I was responsible for building the page and the timeline, reporting, and data analysis.

Built with: HTML, CSS, Timeline.js, CartoDB, D3.js
